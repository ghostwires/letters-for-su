This game was written in the span of... three or four days(?) for the Neo-Interactives Smoochie Jam!

If you want to know more about what Susato's up to in England, or if you like any combination of steampunk, Sherlock Holmes, lawyer simulators, and visual novels with sick music, I cannot recommend it strongly enough.

This is my first ever entry to a game jam, and I hope you enjoy :)
